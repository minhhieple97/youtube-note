YouTube Notes

Take time-stamped notes while watching YouTube videos.

  • Look for the notes panel below the video information
  • Click "Add Note" to create a note at the current timestamp
  • Click on timestamps to quickly jump to that point in the video
  • Export/Import notes for backup

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Create new note Alt + N
  • Save note Ctrl + Enter
  • Cancel editing Esc
